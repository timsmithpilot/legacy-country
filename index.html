THE FAMILY BEHIND THE MUSIC

Meet the Band

Alaina

Vocals • Acoustic Guitar

As the oldest sibling, Alaina naturally helps lead the way. She picked up guitar quickly and pairs confident rhythm playing with a strong lead voice and a natural ear for harmony.

Maggie

Vocals • Piano & Keys

Maggie has played piano since age five. Her gentle personality, clear voice and close harmony with Alaina bring warmth to the Legacy sound.

Luke

Drums

Luke learned the basics from Dad and then made the drums his own. He takes rehearsals seriously and keeps the whole band tight and moving.

Jake

Bass

Jake started bass because the band needed one, then discovered that he loved the groove. He experiments with sounds and serves as the group’s dependable comic relief.

Tim

Guitar • Vocals

Dad started the band and now mostly tries to keep up with the kids—while playing guitar, singing and serving as tour-bus driver because he is the only one old enough to drive.
